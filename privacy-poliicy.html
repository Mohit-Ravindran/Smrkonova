Privacy Policy

Protecting the personal information of our customers is a paramount responsibility at Smrkonova Softech Solutions LLP. We are committed to safeguarding privacy with transparency and choice for our clients and website visitors. This privacy policy outlines the data collection, storage, and processing practices. Please review it to understand how we handle your data, your rights, and how to contact us with any questions. We reserve the right to update this privacy statement at our discretion, and we encourage you to check this section periodically for changes.

Applicable To:

This privacy statement pertains to individuals ("users" or "you") engaging with our website, subscribing to blogs, seeking information, or using chat support.

How We Collect Data:

Depending on your interactions, whether through blogs, information requests, or chat support, we gather data from the following sources:

Data We Request:

  • Your Country
  • Your Email Id

  • Other information you choose to share

We seek and process these data with your consent when you utilize our services.

Cookies, small files on your computer, are used to analyze web traffic. We collect this data with your consent. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

We collect data for various reasons, including communication, service provision, notifications, updates, and enhancing user experience.

We have implemented administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, manipulation, or exposure of your information.
